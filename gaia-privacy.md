## Introduction

By inviting the publicly-hosted instance of Gaia to your Discord server or sending commands to the bot, you acknowledge and agree to the collection and use of information according to the Privacy Policy.

## Information Collection and Use

The bot processes the following data:
- Discord user and server IDs
- Aggregate command usage statistics

The information the bot collects is used only for the purposes of providing and improving the bot's functionality. The information may be retained indefinitely to continue providing the bot's functionality.

The data is stored on servers hosted by [Endless Hosting](https://theendlessweb.com/) in the United States.

## Third-Party Content

The bot may link to third-party content such as Hytale skins or Hytale server listings. The bot has no control over, and assumes no responsibility for any third-party content. If third-party content is violating your privacy, then you should contact the host of that content.

The bot may link to third-party websites. We advise you to read the Privacy Policy of these websites. We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for the privacy policies or practices of any third-party websites.
