This snapshot adds new models for baby horses, donkeys, and mules.

## Baby Mobs

The hitbox size for baby horses, zombie horses, and skeleton horses have changed:

| Width                  | Height     | Eye Height   |
| ---------------------- | ---------- | ------------ |
| 0.6982422 → 0.97753908 | 0.8 → 1.12 | 0.76 → 1.064 |

The hitbox sizes for donkeys and mules have not changed.
