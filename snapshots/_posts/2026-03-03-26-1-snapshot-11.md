A few more tiny tweaks and big backend changes for the Tiny Takeover.

## Dragon Egg

Dragon eggs no longer teleport to positions where the block directly below the dragon egg would be air, fixing [MC-231663](https://bugs.mojang.com/browse/MC-231663).
