Another 26.1 pre-release adds a few significant tweaks.

## Spear

The speed required for an attacker to dismount a mounted entity with a spear has changed:

| Spear     | Required Speed       |
| --------- | -------------------- |
| Wood      | 14 blocks/tick       |
| Stone     | 10 → 13 blocks/tick  |
| Copper    | 9 → 12 blocks/tick   |
| Iron      | 8 → 11 blocks/tick   |
| Golden    | 10 → 13 blocks/tick  |
| Diamond   | 7.5 → 10 blocks/tick |
| Netherite | 7 → 9 blocks/tick    |

## Mobs

Baby mob hitboxes have not changed.

### Witch

If the witch's target is 2 blocks or closer horizontally, the witch will throw potions at 60% speed (power 0.45 instead of 0.75).

### Villager

If a datapack adds a third master librarian trade, it will be available even if the Villager Rebalance experiment is disabled.
