Another 1.19.4 pre-release contains bug fixes and texture tweaks.

# Changes

Only the following mobs force the rider to dismount when underwater:
- Horse
- Zombie Horse
- Donkey
- Mule
- LLama
- Trader Llama
- Camel
- Pig
- Chicken
- Spider
- Ravager
- Strider
