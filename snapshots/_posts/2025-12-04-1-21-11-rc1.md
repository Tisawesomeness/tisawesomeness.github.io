The first 1.21.11 release candidate is out, making spears last longer.

## Spear

The tired and disengaged timings (in seconds) for each spear material have changed:

| Tier      | Delay | Engaged             | Tired                  | Disengaged            |
| --------- | ----- | ------------------- | ---------------------- | --------------------- |
| Wood      | 0.75  | 15.00 (15.75 total) | **10.0 (25.75 total)** | 5.0 **(30.75 total)** |
| Stone     | 0.70  | 13.75 (14.45 total) | **9.0 (23.45 total)**  | 4.5 **(27.95 total)** |
| Copper    | 0.65  | 12.50 (13.15 total) | **8.25 (21.40 total)** | 4.0 **(25.40 total)** |
| Iron      | 0.60  | 11.25 (11.85 total) | **6.75 (18.60 total)** | 2.5 **(21.10 total)** |
| Gold      | 0.70  | 13.75 (14.45 total) | **8.0 (22.45 total)**  | 3.5 **(25.95 total)** |
| Diamond   | 0.50  | 10.00 (10.50 total) | **6.5 (17.00 total)**  | 3.0 **(20.00 total)** |
| Netherite | 0.40  | 8.75 (9.15 total)   | **5.5 (14.65 total)**  | 2.5 **(17.15 total)** |
